Pork Chops Recipe

This recipe will teach you how to cook big back prok chops.

Ingredients

  • Pork Back Chop
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • BBQ Sauce

Instructions

  1. Wash Pork Back Chops.
  2. Spill Salt and Pepper onto Pork Chops.
  3. Pan-fry the Chops.
  4. Let sit for 10 minutes.
